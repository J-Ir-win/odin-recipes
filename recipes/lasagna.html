Lasagna recipe

Lasagna is a type of pasta made in wide, flat sheets. Made by stacking layers of pasta and baked in dish

Ingredients

  • Italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • sugar
  • fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Pasta sheets
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • Eggs

Making the Lasagna

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the pasta sheets
  3. Layer the lasagna
  4. Cover with foil and bake
  5. Leave to rest beofre serving
